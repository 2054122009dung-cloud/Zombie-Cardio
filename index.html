OUTBREAK WALK

A Real-World Survival Loop

The signal's dead. Your phone is the only thing standing between you and them. Walk to scavenge. Run to escape. Fight only when cornered. Every step in the real world moves you through the wasteland. Every meter matters.

FIELD MANUAL

① MOVEMENT
Your GPS speed drives the game. Walk slowly to scavenge safely. Jog/run to flee a horde — but running burns Stamina fast.

② HANDS & COMBOS
Tap lower-left / lower-right zones to swing with that hand. Alternating L-R-L-R builds a COMBO multiplier for bonus damage. A held swing (tap & hold ~0.4s) is a HEAVY attack — more damage, more durability loss, more stamina.

③ BLOCK & DODGE
Hold the center BLOCK button to raise your weapon/shield and reduce incoming damage. Tap the small middle-bottom zone to DODGE (sidestep) — perfectly timed against a telegraphed lunge, you take zero damage.

④ TELEGRAPHS
Zombies flash a warning ring before they lunge. React: block, dodge, or counter-swing during the window.

⑤ SCAVENGING
As you walk, the world generates nearby supply markers from your real GPS movement. Reach the marker's distance to loot — food, water, melee weapons, gun ammo, bandages.

⑥ HORDE TYPES
Shamblers are slow & weak. Runners close distance fast and attack quickly. Brutes hit hard and shrug off light weapons.

⑦ INJURY
Pressing an attack instead of disengaging raises your "Emotion" meter — high heat = high limb-injury risk. A hurt arm disables that hand's weapon. A hurt leg permanently caps speed & stamina regen for the run.

⑧ GUNS
Limited ammo, emergency only — best against Brutes or when surrounded.

GEAR CHECK

OUTBREAK WALK needs Location access to track your real walking/running speed, and (on supported phones) Motion/Orientation for the first-person look-around. Go outside, hold your phone naturally, and keep the screen on.

If permissions are denied or unavailable (e.g. desktop), a simulated walk/run mode will be used instead so you can still test the game.
