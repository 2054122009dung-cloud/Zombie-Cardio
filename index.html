OUTBREAK WALK

GPS · Combat · Survival

The signal's dead. Walk to scavenge. Run to break contact. Fight only when cornered. Your real-world movement drives everything — no buttons to move, only your legs.

FIELD MANUAL

① MOVEMENT
Your GPS speed drives the world. Walk to scavenge safely. Running builds distance and burns stamina — but escaping a horde requires real running.

② SWING ZONES
Tap the left or right half of the screen to swing that hand. Alternating builds a COMBO multiplier. Hold longer for a HEAVY attack — more damage, more stamina.

③ BLOCK & DODGE
Hold BLOCK to reduce incoming damage. Tap the small centre-bottom zone to DODGE — perfectly timed on a telegraph, you take zero damage.

④ TELEGRAPHS
Zombies flash a warning ring before lunging. Counter, block, or dodge in that window.

⑤ TYPES
Shamblers — slow & weak. Runners — fast & relentless. Brutes — heavy HP, shrug light weapons.

⑥ INJURY
A hurt arm disables that hand. A hurt leg caps speed & stamina regen — permanently for this run.

⑦ DESKTOP / NO-GPS MODE
If GPS isn't available, a simulated walk/run starts automatically — the world moves but no movement buttons are shown.

CHOOSE YOUR SURVIVOR

Each background reshapes your stamina, loadout, and how the dead react to you.

GEAR CHECK

OUTBREAK WALK uses GPS to track your real walking speed. Go outside, hold your phone naturally, and keep the screen on.

If GPS is denied or unavailable (desktop/indoor), a simulation will move you through the zone automatically so you can still fight.
